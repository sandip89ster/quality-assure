FAQ

Can you integrate into our CI/CD?

Yes. We commonly use GitHub Actions and Jenkins to run suites on every PR and nightly. We’ll add artifacts and dashboards (e.g., Allure).

Which browsers and devices do you support?

Chromium, Firefox, WebKit/Safari; Android & iOS with a mix of emulators and real devices. Cloud device labs available on request.

Do you sign NDAs and follow SOC2/GDPR practices?

Absolutely. We’re comfortable with NDAs, least-privilege access, and data minimization aligned with GDPR.

Can you start this week?

Typical kickoff is within 5–7 business days. For audits and short cycles, we can often begin sooner.
